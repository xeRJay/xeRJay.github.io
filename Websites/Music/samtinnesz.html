Sam Tinnesz is a singer/songwriter from Nashville, TN.


Tinnesz used to be the guitarist for MIKESCHAIR and is credited for writing for many well-known artists such as Skillet, Jordan Feliz, VERIDIA, and more.


As a singer, Tinnesz has worked on many projects. His work as a solo artist has been featured in many television promotional and movie trailers, leading up to his 2017 debut album, Babel.
